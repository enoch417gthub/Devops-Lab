What is DevOps? Devops is da Best

DevOps is a combination of Development and Operations. It is a culture and set of practices that helps teams build, test, and release software faster and more reliably. DevOps focuses on automation, collaboration, monitoring, and continuous improvement.

In DevOps, developers and IT operations work together to reduce errors, deploy faster, and maintain stable systems.
